Application Information

Please note that the following documentation is required to complete the form:

  • 2 x Certified Unabridged birth certificates/study permit/refugee status/passport
  • Recent I.D. size photograph of child
  • Most recent school report & copy of latest School Fee Statement & Fee Clearance Form
  • Clinic card showing the relevant immunisations
  • Certified copies of Identity Documents of both biological parents (Should either parent be deceased, a certified copy of the death certificate is required)
  • Certified copy of marriage certificate/divorce agreement (from the court)
  • In the case of Legal Guardianship/Adoption ~ certified copies of legal documents produced by the Courts are required
  • Proof of residence of the biological parents ~ Municipality Account (original municipal account - Affidavit NOT accepted)
  • Copy of medical aid membership card (if applicable)
  • Proof of Employment/Letter from Department of Labour (if unemployed) for both parents

Note: For a learner to be eligible for admission to this school, they must comply with the admissions criteria as determined by the South African Schools Act and regulations. Admission will not be finalized until all relevant documentation has been received by Benjamin Pine Primary School.
